# Dynegy (dynegy)

Dynegy (https://www.dynegy.com) is a retail electric service provider and part of the Vistra Corp family of companies. Dynegy delivers simple, price-protected retail electricity plans for homes and businesses across the Northeast, Mid-Atlantic, and Midwest, including Ohio, Illinois, and Pennsylvania.

This repository tracks the developer resources, APIs, and machine-readable artifacts associated with Dynegy.

## Overview

Dynegy operates as a retail electric provider in deregulated electricity markets, offering fixed-rate and variable-rate plans for residential and commercial customers. Dynegy is one of several retail electric brands operated by Vistra Corp, alongside TXU Energy, Homefield Energy, Ambit, and U.S. Gas & Electric.

## APIs

No public APIs have been documented for Dynegy. The company operates a customer-facing account portal for billing, payments, and plan management, but no public developer portal, REST API, or programmatic integration documentation has been published.
